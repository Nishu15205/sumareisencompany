    Golden Triangle Tour

    A tour of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, often referred to as the “Golden Triangle” tour, offers a rich tapestry of India’s cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and vibrant cities.

    Delhi: The capital city of India, Delhi is a blend of ancient and modern. Key attractions include the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, India Gate, and the Lotus Temple. Visitors can explore bustling markets like Chandni Chowk and enjoy the diverse culinary scene.

    Agra: Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, Agra is a city steeped in Mughal history. Other significant sites include Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri. The city offers a glimpse into the architectural marvels and rich history of the Mughal era.

    Jaipur: Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is renowned for its majestic forts and palaces. Highlights include the Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Jantar Mantar. Jaipur is also famous for its vibrant markets, where visitors can shop for traditional Rajasthani crafts and jewelry.

    Together, these cities provide a comprehensive experience of India’s diverse cultural and historical heritage.

    Tour Schedule


    Day 01 Arrive Delhi
    Upon arrival, after clearing immigration and custom, you will be met and transferred to your hotel. (Check-in at 1400hrs) Overnight at hotel

    Day 02 Delhi
    After breakfast, full day city tour of Old & New Delhi
    Old Delhi: drive past Red Fort, Visit Raj Ghat, Jama Masjid, and Chandni Chowk Afternoon, visit New Delhi. Humayun’s Tomb, Qutab Minar and Continue with a drive through Embassy area, the Government buildings (1921-1930) Overnight at hotel

    Day 03 Delhi / Agra (205Kms /5hrs approx.)
    After breakfast, drive to Agra.
    Upon arrival, check in to your hotel.
    Afternoon, visit Taj Mahal and Agra Fort city tour. Overnight at hotel

    Day 04 Agra /Fatehpur Sikri / Jaipur (256Kms / 5hrs 30mins approx.)
    After breakfast, drive to Jaipur en-route visit Fatehpur Sikri
    After visit, continue your drive to Jaipur, upon arrival, check in to your hotel. Overnight at hotel

    Day 05 Jaipur After breakfast, visit Amber Fort
    Afternoon, visit Jaipur City: Hawa Mahal, City Palace & Observatory and Solar Observatory. Overnight at hotel

    Day 06 Jaipur / Delhi Depart (260Kms /6hrs approx.)
    Morning free (Check out 1200hrs)
    After breakfast drive to Delhi. Upon arrival, transfer to international airport for your flight back home. (No Accommodation in Delhi)
