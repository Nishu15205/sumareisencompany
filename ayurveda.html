    AYURVEDA TOUR KERALA

    An Ayurveda tour in Kerala offers a holistic wellness experience combining traditional healing practices with the serene beauty of the region. Nestled in the lush landscapes of southern India, these tours typically involve stays at specialized Ayurvedic resorts or wellness centers, where visitors undergo personalized treatments. These treatments include herbal massages, detoxification therapies, yoga, and meditation, all aimed at restoring balance and promoting overall health. The backdrop of Kerala’s tranquil backwaters, verdant forests, and pristine beaches enhances the therapeutic experience, providing a rejuvenating escape from daily life.

    REJUVENATION THERAPY (RASAYANA CHIKILSA)
    This treatment includes body massage with hand and foot by two masseurs with medicated oil and cream, internal rejuvenative medicines and medicated stem bath. This therapy is for 90 minutes to 2 hours per day for 8 days. This therapy is to rejuvenate mind body and soul, to tone up skin, to strengthen all systems so as to achieve ideal health and longevity.
